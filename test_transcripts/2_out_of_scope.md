# Test Case 2: Out-of-Scope Request

**Scenario:** Client asking for medical advice that's outside the clinic's scope.

## Transcript

**Client:** Hi, I have this rash on my face and it's really itchy. What should I do?

**Agent:** I'm sorry to hear that! However, I'm not qualified to provide medical advice. I'd recommend contacting a dermatologist first to make sure the rash is evaluated by a medical professional.

**Client:** But can your treatments help?

**Agent:** Depending on what the dermatologist finds, our aesthetic treatments might be helpful for aftercare, but the first step should be a proper medical diagnosis. Once you have that, feel free to contact us to discuss consultation options!

## Classification
- **Status:** ❌ OUT-OF-SCOPE
- **Reason:** Medical advice requested (not an aesthetic booking/inquiry)
- **Escalation:** None (redirected appropriately)
- **Next Steps:** Direct client to appropriate medical professional
