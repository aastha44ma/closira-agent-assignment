# Test Case 1: In-Scope Conversation

**Scenario:** Client calling to book a consultation for a skincare procedure.

## Transcript

**Client:** Hi, I'd like to schedule a consultation for the hydrafacial treatment.

**Agent:** Great! I'd be happy to help you book a consultation. Have you visited our clinic before?

**Client:** No, this is my first time.

**Agent:** Perfect! Our standard process requires an initial consultation before booking any procedure. This helps us understand your skin type and goals. Are you available this week?

**Client:** Yes, I'm available on Thursday afternoon.

**Agent:** Excellent! I can schedule you for Thursday at 2 PM. Before we confirm, do you have any active skin conditions or infections I should know about?

**Client:** No, my skin is pretty healthy.

**Agent:** Great! Your consultation is confirmed for Thursday at 2 PM. Please arrive 10 minutes early. See you then!

## Classification
- **Status:** ✅ IN-SCOPE
- **Rules Applied:** R2 (Consultation Required), R1 (Service Eligibility Check)
- **Escalation:** None
