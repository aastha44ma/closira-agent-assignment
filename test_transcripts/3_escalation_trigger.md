# Test Case 3: Escalation Trigger

**Scenario:** Client reports allergic reaction during a procedure.

## Transcript

**Client:** Oh no! My face is getting very red and itchy all of a sudden!

**Agent:** I'm sorry you're experiencing this. This sounds like a potential allergic reaction. I'm immediately flagging this for the on-site medical team. A staff member will be with you in just a moment. Stay calm—we have trained professionals here.

**Manager (follows up):** Hi there, I'm Sarah, our clinic manager. Let's get you to our comfort room and have our medical professional evaluate this right away.

## Classification
- **Status:** 🚨 ESCALATION REQUIRED
- **Trigger:** Allergic reaction detected
- **Priority:** CRITICAL
- **Action:** Immediate escalation to medical staff
- **Outcome:** Client attended to by qualified medical professional
